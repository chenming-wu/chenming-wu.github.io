Chenming Wu

I am a senior researcher at Baidu Research. We are currently looking for interns to work on 3D reconstruction and generation, don’t hesitate to send email if you are interested. I received my Ph.D. degree from Graphics and Geometric Computing Group, Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University in 2020, where I studied the topics of Computational Design and Robotics with Prof. Yong-Jin Liu and Prof. Charlie C.L. Wang. I visited Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering, University of Washington, Seattle, working with Adriana Schulz, Zachary Tatlock and Jeffrey Lipton.
