I am currently working at Baidu Inc.. I previously worked at Tencent and interned at AiFi Inc.. I received my Ph.D. degree from Graphics and Geometric Computing Group, Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University in 2020. I visited the Graphics and Imaging Lab, Paul G. Allen School of CSE, University of Washington (Seattle, WA) in 2019, and Department of Design Engineering of Delft University of Technology (Netherlands) in 2017. My research interest lies in computational design, video/3D generation, and robotics.

We are actively looking for full-time employees and interns to work on reconstruction and generation, send me an email if you are interested.
