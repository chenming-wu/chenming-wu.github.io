I am currently working at Baidu Inc. We are actively looking for interns to work on 3D AIGC and Reconstruction, please send me an email if you are interested. I previously worked at Tencent and interned at AiFi Inc. I received my Ph.D. degree from Graphics and Geometric Computing Group, Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University in 2020. I visited the Graphics and Imaging Lab, Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering, University of Washington, Seattle in 2019. My research interest lies in computational design, 3D content generation, and robotics.
